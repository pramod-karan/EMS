About Us

G-Force Management Services was founded in 2009 and provides professional human resource solutions to more than 100+ companies and their 2500+ employees. We focus our solutions on those companies in Bengalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata & Delhi. but we are licensed to provide our services in other states also. By maintaining this regional focus, we offer an unmatched level of professional services to our clients and their employees.

G-Force Management Services is a division of K-Marks, one of the top 10 commercial staffing companies in India. With over 10 years experience and 10+ branch locations, Karan Group provides specialized staffing solutions to business across the country. In 2015, K-Marks became part of Recruit Holdings Co., Ltd., the send largest EMS firm in the country. We are proud to be a Human Resource Services leader in the world committed to customer service.
Your business is just that, your business. You maintain complete control of:
  • Managing and directing the day-to-day business operations and worksite employees in the creation and delivery of your products and services.
  • Providing worksite employees with the tools, instruments, and safe place of work.
  • Aligning and supporting the employee and employer relationship.
  • Full-service payroll and required tax processing for your business.
  • Approving and endorsing company policies and procedures.
  • Approving all benefit programs offered to the worksite employees.
