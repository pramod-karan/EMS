About Us

Employee Management Services was founded in 1994 and provides professional human resource solutions to more than 160 companies and their 5,500+ employees. We focus our solutions on those companies in Ohio, Kentucky, and Indiana, but we are licensed to provide our services in other states. By maintaining this regional focus, we offer an unmatched level of professional services to our clients and their employees.

Employee Management Services is a division of Staffmark, one of the top 10 commercial staffing companies in the United States. With over 30 years experience and 300+ branch locations, Staffmark provides specialized staffing solutions to business across the country. In 2011, Staffmark became part of Recruit Holdings Co., Ltd., the fourth largest staffing firm in the world. We are proud to be a Human Resource Services leader in the world committed to customer service.
